Queens Continues to Top Other Boroughs in Positive COVID Cases

As the coronavirus continues to ravage the New York City area, Queens is taking the brunt of it as cases continue to rise throughout the borough. New York City Department of Health has kept the public updated on positive cases throughout the five boroughs, however, Queens has been leading. Governor Andrew Cuomo announced 20 zipcodes COVID hotspots through the New York City area and identified these three zip codes in Queens:11367 (Kew Garden Hill), 11691( Far Rockaway), and 11432 (Jamaica Hills). Cuomo gave these zipcodes restrictions for a number of weeks which caused a drop in cases, but once they were lifted cases rose once more. Recently, Cuomo announced that all of New York City will have restaurants and other businesses close at 10 pm and have a limit of 10 people indoors for events. Queens will have to see if this will help their growing positive cases in the long run.
